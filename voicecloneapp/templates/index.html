What is SPEAK

Voice cloning, also known as voice replication or voice synthesis, is the process of creating a digital replica of a person's voice. There are several approaches to voice cloning, but they all involve using machine learning algorithms and speech synthesis techniques to generate a synthetic voice that sounds like the original person. Use a deep neural network-based speaker verification system as a pre-trained model and fine-tune it for the task of TTS synthesis.
